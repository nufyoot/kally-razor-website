About KallyRazor

Coming from the .NET world, I've always really enjoyed using Razor to create my web sites. When I finally decided to give Node.js a try I really wanted something similar to Razor. There are many alternatives that exist, but none of them quite fit what I wanted. The beauty of being a programmer is filling in the gaps. Not just contributing to projects that you think are promising, but creating new ones that fill a need for you and possibly others.

I may be alone in this journey, but that's OK. I'm truly just here to learn and to provide myself with a tool that fills a gap I have.
